Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about COIS, enrollment, and payments.

How do I enroll a student?

To enroll, choose a program under "Our Programs" and click "Enroll Now". You will be guided to the payment section to complete registration.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Credit Card, Bank Transfer, and Mobile Pay. Visit the Payments section for step-by-step instructions.

Are scholarships or financial aid available?

Yes — COIS offers limited scholarships and payment plans. Contact our admissions team via the Contact Us page for eligibility details.

How long are the programs?

Program durations vary by track. Visit each program's page under "Our Programs" for specific duration and curriculum details.

How can I get further support?

For additional help, use the Help Center or Contact Us links in the footer, or email support@cois.example (replace with your real support address).
